### Cross-Linking Strategy

**Service Pages → Location Pages**
- Service page links to relevant location pages where that service is offered
- Example: `/services/website-development` → `/locations/india`, `/locations/usa`

**Industry Pages → Service Pages**
- Industry page shows which services apply to that industry
- Example: `/industries/real-estate` → `/services/website-development`, `/services/google-ads-management`

**City Pages → Industry Pages**
- City page shows industries served in that city
- Example: `/cities/delhi` → `/industries/real-estate-delhi`, `/industries/law-firms-delhi`

**Blog → Landing Pages**
- Blog articles link to relevant service/industry/location pages
- Example: Blog post on "SEO for Law Firms" → `/industries/law-firms` → `/industry-services/seo-law-firms`

**Case Studies → Services/Industries**
- Case studies linked from relevant service and industry pages
- Example: Real estate case study on `/industries/real-estate` page

**Comparison Pages → Service/Alternative Pages**
- Comparison page links to both options being compared
- Example: "WordPress vs Custom" → Links to both WordPress and custom development service pages

**Resource Pages → Blog**
- Guides/resources link to related blog posts for deeper content
- Example: "Web Development Guide" → Links to 10-15 related blog posts
